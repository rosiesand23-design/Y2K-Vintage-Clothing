about the shop

Slow fashion, one treasure at a time.

fairytqles is a small curated vintage shop. Every piece is thrifted by hand, cleaned, measured, and photographed before it's listed — so what you see is exactly what arrives.

Because everything is secondhand, each item is one of one. If something speaks to you, it won't be restocked. All purchases, questions, and offers happen through Depop.

🌷

hand-picked
